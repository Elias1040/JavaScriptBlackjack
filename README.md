## Intro
Blakjack is a final exams assignment which is a javascript based blackjack game, in my application there is singleplayer and multiplayer blackjack

## Instructions

##### Singleplayer
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/101317597/193152486-861e3f23-2dcd-43ab-b7d6-13d7bfa1f404.png)
* Dealers hand: the top hand is the dealers hand
* Players hand: the bottom hand is the players hand
* Hit: draw a card if the sum is above 21 you lose
* Stand: dealer draws his cards and all points will be calculated and the result screen appears

##### Multiplayer
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/101317597/193152369-3147eb42-b91f-4146-aa40-502c525b1fe1.png)
* Dealers hand: the top hand is the dealers hand
* Players hand: the bottom left hand is the players hand
* Other players hand: the bottom right hand is the other players hand
* Hit: draw a card if the sum is above 21 you lose
* Stand: dealer draws his cards and all points will be calculated and the result screen appears
